Our project would make ordering food easier for the users. This web- and mobile application would offer people food / restaurant recommendations based on their location and preferences in the style that resembles the Instagram "for you" page.

There would be pictures and videos of meals, food making videos, and after the user clicked on a picture of a meal, the app would show the name of the meal, the ingredients and the price and the distance of the restaurant. There would be the option to order the food, and the user could choose from different food delivery services (Foodora, Foodpanda, etc.).
