The project idea is about a learning-platform, kind of similar to the TeachCenter of the university, but with the advantage of having all information and actions for a course at one place, for having a better overview and more convenience.

Also we would implement an improved version of the Safe-Exam-Browser, that is operating-system-independent, and offers a better usability.

We also provide extensive statistical analyses from assignments and exams conducted in our learning-platform (analysis probably with LLMS).

Finally, the system will include a personalized study program feature that tracks individual progress and delivers targeted feedback, helping each student optimize their learning journey.
