Our software will scan medial reports, which users can upload an image or PDF. Afterwards the system will store the data and generate the list of diseases and injuries.

It can be connected to a smartwatch to track heart rate, and other parameters.

Notifies you when it's optimal to visit a medical professional, assessing the severity of your health condition and advises when self-treatment is necessary.

The target market will be the U.S. as the U.S. healthcare system is always subject to a charge.

We provide viable option for decision making when to take action as U.S. medical expenses could severely impact your finances.
