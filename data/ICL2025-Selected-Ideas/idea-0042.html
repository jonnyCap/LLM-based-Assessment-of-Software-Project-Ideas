Bus ticket selling service
Our platform is a one-stop web app that streamlines intercity and international travel, starting with bus tickets and designed for future expansion into air and train travel. Users can easily search for bus tickets with detailed listings of routes, bus companies, departure/arrival times, and pricing. Acting as a trusted intermediary, the app manages a smooth checkout process using fast, secure payment options like Apple Pay and PayPal. Additionally, it features curated travel agency offers for your chosen destination via referral links, while also collecting valuable user search data—such as destinations, travel dates, and number of passengers—to help travel agencies tailor their services.
