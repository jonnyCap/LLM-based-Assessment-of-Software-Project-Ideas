Climate change is one of the biggest challenges of our time! Our world is precious but we don't care as much as we should! Therefore we asked ourselves a few questions:

  1. Why is there so much waste of food?
  2. Why do people don't make sustainable choices?
  3. Why do people deny the climate change?
  4. Why is a greener future so circuitous?
  5. What does it take to incentivize eco-friendly behavior to everyone?

While many people and businesses act already sustainable, there are unfortunately a lot of people who have little motivation for doing so. Most of the times it fails because of the idea to barely effect the CO2 emissions by doing odds-and-ends as a single person.

"Count on me!" is an app designed to promote climate protection by rewarding the participants who track their sustainable actions to reduce their carbon footprint. We want not only try to make sustainability more attractive than ever, but make it user friendly as possible by creating an app for your smartphone. It converts your daily actions such as public transport, buying secondhand, or reducing waste into rewards like discounts, or vouchers for your locals. Every little step counts and together we can do something memorable! Let's count on us and appreciate sustainability together!

How does it work? We collaborate with companies which offers possibilities to reduce the carbon footprint by buying specific products from them. This could be for example bread getting stale at the local bakery, food close to the expiration date, secondhand products or even train tickets. Our participants then receive points for these purchases and can redeem them next time. This leads to less waste, more public transport users and giving products a second chance!
