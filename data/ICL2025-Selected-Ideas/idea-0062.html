DormOnlineOnline System for elections and polls made for students dormitories

Problem:

• Inefficiency • some dorms lack space to gather larger groups • Only a low amount of participants • Limited time (residents and organizers) • Limited Resources

Participating in decisions is important, as the result determines: • Budget allocation • Event specifics • Changes in Rules and Obligations And much more

Solution:

Online Platform, with which each resident can participate in elections and polls. Due to easier access, there will be more feedback, participation, interest from residents in taking active part in shaping the dorm together.

Benefits

• More flexibility and easier access for voters • High amount of participants • More transperancy

Features

  1. Secure User Authentication: Each user can login with their personal credentials. Users are prohibited in having more than 1 account.
  2. Elections (incl. candidate profiles): Each user has 1 vote for a role, this is technically enforced. Candidates are portraited (with or without a profile picture) with a short description aboout them and their goals. The election is open for a limited timeframe.
  3. Polls: Polls can be single or multiple choice. They are open for a limited timeframe.
  4. Real Time Results and Final Results: Each User (Login may be required) can ciew a dashboard with live results.
  5. Admin Dashboard: Administrators can create elections, polls.
