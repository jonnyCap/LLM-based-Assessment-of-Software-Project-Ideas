Our app is made for pet owners to help them manage and improve their pets’ health and well-being. 

It allows users to store important information like medical records, vaccination details, and health check-up history. The app uses AI to provide personalized insights based on the species and breed of the pet, including information on common allergies, breed-specific conditions, and care tips. Users can set reminders for upcoming vet appointments or treatments and even book appointments through the app.

 Additionally, the app features a community platform where pet owners can connect, share experiences, and offer advice. Another useful feature includes suggested walking routes based on the user’s location.
