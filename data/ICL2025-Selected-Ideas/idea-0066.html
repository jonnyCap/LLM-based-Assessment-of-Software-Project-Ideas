Our project is a Lifestyle/Fitness App. The App can track your sleep/nutrition and give you a feedback. The app can give you a specific plan depending on your fitness goal. It can give you a plan depending on your day activity such as work, school, age, free time and other circumstances. If you purchase the premium version you can book a session with a certified fitness expert. You can add friends, check your progress and collect points by achieving your fitness tasks and compete with others.
